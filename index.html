Harry Potter is a series of fantasy novels created by J.K. Rowling.The novels tell the story of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, that struggle against Lord Voldemort, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal, overthrow the wizard governing body known as the Ministry of Magic and subjugate all wizards and Muggles (non-magical people).
The world created by the author is very complex, so this webpage will help you understand a little bit more about the more common creatures, spells and characters.
