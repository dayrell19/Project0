Harry Potter

The boy who lived. He's one of the most talented wizards of his generation. He destroyed two of the seven horcrux of Voldemort.

Hermione Granger

She's one of Harry's best friends and the smartest witch of her generation. She destroyed Hufflepuff's cup horcrux.

Ron Weasley

He is Harry's best friend. He was the one who destroyed Slytherin's locker horcrux. Ended up as an auror in the Ministry of Magic.

Alvo Dumbledore

He's the greatest wizard of all time.Before passing away, he was the director of Hogwarts and the only wizard Voldemort feared.

Severus Snape

He was Harry's potion professor in Hogwarts. He was a double-side agent, spying on Voldemort for Dumbledore. He was killed by Voldemort and Nagini.

Lord Voldemort

He is the greatest dark wizard of all time. He was the only person in history of being able to create more then one horcrux. He was killed by Harry Potter.
