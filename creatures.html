Acromantula

It's a species of giant spiders that were used for treasure hoards. They are incredibly smart and are very aggressive

Basilisk

It's a species of giant snakes. Anything that looks straight to its eyes die. It is a very agressive animal.

Dementor

They are the guards of Azkaban. They feed on happiness and generate feelings of depression in anyone around them.

Hippogriff

It's a mythological creature with head of a eagle and body of a horse. It is a very proud animal so needs to be approached with extreme care.

Thunderbird

It is a mythological bird that is able to control the weather and create storms. It is a very rare creature that lives in North America.

Thestral

It is a creature that is invisible to everyone except those who have seen death. They are very gentle creatures but people avoid them because of their looks.
