Expelliarmus

A spell that disarms the enemy. It is the main spell of Harry Potter
Stupefy

A spell that knocks down the enemy. Teached by Harry to Dumbledores Army in Order of Phoenix
Avada Kedavra

One of the three unforgivable curses. This spell kills the enemy instantaneously
Crucio

One of the three unforgivable curses. This spell tortures the enemy
Imperius

One of the three unforgivable curses. This spell allows you to control your enemy.
Alohomora

This spell unlocks doors. Used by hermione in Sorcerer Stone
Expecto Patronum

This spell generates a shield of good energy in form of white light. A good mechanism against dementors
Petrificus Totalus

Its a spell that petrifies the enemy. Used by Neville Longbottom in the end of Sorcerer Stone
Sectumsempra

Created by Severus Snape, this spell cuts the enemy multiple times. Used by Harry in the Half-Blood Prince
Vulnera Sanentur

It is the counter spell for the Sectumsempra. It heals the wounds created by the other spell.
Protego

It is a defensive spell. It creates a shield against most of the spells. It does not protect you from the unforgivable curses.
Legilimens

Spell used by Snape in Harry in Order of Phoenix. It allows you to get inside of the enemys head and see its memories.
